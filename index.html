The process

From market to pouch, in order

Every batch follows the same four steps — no shortcuts, no shared equipment with anything fried or preserved in syrup.

01

Harvest

Sourced the same week from local markets and growers — never held in cold storage for weeks first.

02

Slice

Cut thin and even by hand, so every piece dries at the same rate.

03

Dehydrate

Low, steady heat for six to ten hours. Never fried, never blanched in syrup.

04

Pack

Sealed the same day with a food-grade silica sachet, weighed and batch-dated.
